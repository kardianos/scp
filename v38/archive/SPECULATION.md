# V38 Speculation: Scale Hierarchy and the Braid-as-Electron Hypothesis

## The Original Braid Was Better

The original braid (not braid3) — the infinite helical tube from V33/V34 —
exhibited properties that nothing since has matched:

1. **Self-sustaining**: The traveling wave continuously reconstructs V(P) binding
   at each point. "Self-reconstruction through motion" (V26 Discovery 1).

2. **EMF-like angular field**: The theta (angle) field generated by the moving
   braid looked EXACTLY like an electromagnetic field around a current-carrying
   wire. The curl coupling η×curl(φ) produces a pattern that follows the
   right-hand rule — the braid is the "current," theta is the "magnetic field."

3. **Charge from winding**: The winding number of the braid determines the sign
   of the theta pattern. Same-winding braids attract (+27%), opposite repel
   (-57%). This IS electromagnetism, emergent from the field dynamics.

4. **Non-breathing in special configurations**: V26 Discovery 2 showed that
   mode 2 (triple product only, no m²φ²) does NOT breathe — ω=0.07 (DC).
   This is fundamentally different from the oscillon (ω=1.37).

## The Braid Is Not a Proton — It's an Electron

**Speculation**: The helical braid is an ELECTRON, not a proton/neutron.

Evidence:
- The braid carries a single charge (winding number ±1)
- It generates an EM-like field that follows Maxwell-like dynamics
- It moves through the field medium like a current through a wire
- The theta field response IS the photon field (massless, propagating)
- The braid is fundamentally 1D (a line/tube), not 3D (a sphere)

If the braid is an electron, then:
- **Quarks** would be sub-structures we haven't probed — perhaps localized
  twists or knots WITHIN the braid that carry fractional winding
- **Protons** would be composite objects made of THREE braids bound together
  — but at a MUCH smaller scale than our current grid can resolve
- **The compact structure search (V37) was operating at the wrong scale**
  — we were trying to find a proton-sized object on a grid that can barely
  resolve a single electron-braid

## Scale Hierarchy Problem

Current simulation parameters:
- Grid: N=128, L=15 → dx=0.24 code units
- Braid tube radius: ~2 code units (8 grid cells)
- Braid length: ~10 code units (extends across box)

If the braid is an electron (mass 0.511 MeV) and proton mass is 938 MeV:
- Mass ratio: 1836:1
- Size ratio (classical): ~1836:1 (electron is 1836× larger classically)
- BUT in QM, the electron is pointlike and the proton has radius ~0.84 fm

In our theory:
- The braid (electron) has extent ~10 code units
- A proton (three quarks) would need to fit INSIDE that tube, at scale ~0.005 code units
- At dx=0.24, we can't resolve features smaller than ~0.5 code units
- **We need 100× finer resolution to even BEGIN to see quark structure**

This means:
- N=128 with L=15: proton invisible (sub-grid)
- N=1024 with L=1: MIGHT see proton structure (dx=0.002)
- N=4096 with L=0.1: could resolve quarks (dx=0.00005)

## Key Questions for V38

1. **What exactly makes the braid self-sustaining?** The traveling wave
   reconstructs V(P) at each step. Can we quantify this mechanism precisely?
   What is the minimum speed for self-reconstruction?

2. **Is the theta field truly Maxwell-like?** Does it satisfy ∇·E=ρ, ∇×B=J
   in some limit? The curl coupling gives wave-like propagation, but is the
   charge coupling correct?

3. **What happens at braid-braid interaction?** V34 showed charge-dependent
   force. But does it produce BOUND STATES? Two braids orbiting each other
   would be a meson analog. Three braids in a triangle would be a baryon.

4. **Can we see sub-braid structure?** At very high resolution (N=1024+ on
   GPU), does the braid interior show any fine structure, or is it smooth
   all the way down?

5. **What is the evolutionary oscillon?** The V37 compact structure found by
   evolutionary search is NOT a braid — it's a different kind of object.
   What are its properties? Is it a scalar meson? A glueball? Something new?
