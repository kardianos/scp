## A. Composite Hopfions & Composition Rules

### [01] Hopfion rings in a cubic chiral magnet
**Tags**: `hopfion`, `experimental`, `composition`, `condensed-matter`
- **Summary**: First direct observation of hopfions in FeGe. Coupled hopfion-skyrmion string states with (Q,H) classification. Linking between components contributes to total Hopf charge.
- **Authors**: F. Zheng, N.S. Kiselev, F.N. Rybakov, J. Yang, W. Du, S. Blugel, R.E. Dunin-Borkowski
- **Journal**: Nature 623, 718--723 (2023)
- **DOI**: 10.1038/s41586-023-06658-5
- **URL**: https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-023-06658-5

### [02] JNR Skyrmions
**Tags**: `composition`, `Skyrme`, `multi-soliton`
- **Summary**: New superposition formula for multi-Skyrmion configurations from JNR instanton ansatz. N+1 weighted points produce charge-N Skyrme fields. Energies within few percent of numerical solutions.
- **Authors**: P.M. Sutcliffe
- **Journal**: arXiv:2409.05058 (2024)
- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.05058

### [03] Locations of JNR Skyrmions
**Tags**: `composition`, `Skyrme`
- **Summary**: Geometric and algebraic characterization of constituent skyrmion positions within JNR ansatz.
- **Authors**: J. Cork, M. Sheridan
- **Journal**: arXiv:2505.00075 (2025)
- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.00075

### [04] Confinement-Induced Metastability and Structural Diversity of Hopfions
**Tags**: `hopfion`, `composition`, `condensed-matter`
- **Summary**: Four distinct hopfion types in chiral magnetic films. Bag-like nesting: outer Q_H=0 hopfion encapsulates inner Q_H=+/-1 hopfion. Negative energy density at periphery drives repulsion.
- **Authors**: A.O. Leonov, K. Shigenaga
- **Journal**: arXiv:2511.22846 (2025)
- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.22846

### [05] The shapes of knots corresponding to special Hopfions
**Tags**: `hopfion`, `knot`, `composition`
- **Summary**: Hopfion charge Q = W_1 * W_2 for torus knots. Writhing/twisting numbers constrained by (W_1, W_2). Knot shapes not equivalent to hopfion shapes at same Q.
- **Authors**: T. Chen, S. Yang, T. Li
- **Journal**: Eur. Phys. J. C 83, 691 (2023)
- **DOI**: 10.1140/epjc/s10052-023-11856-6
- **URL**: https://link.springer.com/article/10.1140/epjc/s10052-023-11856-6

### [06] Excitable and Magnetic Knots
**Tags**: `hopfion`, `knot`, `composition`

## C. BPS Skyrme Models & Binding

### [11] Near-BPS Skyrmions
**Tags**: `BPS`, `binding`, `Skyrme`
- **Summary**: BPS limit (L₆ + V only) gives E = E_FB exactly with zero classical binding. Perturbation away from BPS introduces binding at O(epsilon²). BPS solitons are compactons.
- **Authors**: C. Adam, A. Naya, J. Oles, T. Romanczukiewicz, A. Wereszczynski
- **Journal**: JHEP 2022(11), 092 (2022)
- **DOI**: 10.1007/JHEP11(2022)092
- **URL**: https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/JHEP11(2022)092

### [12] Quantum binding energies in the Skyrme model
**Tags**: `BPS`, `binding`, `Skyrme`, `composition`
- **Summary**: Vibrational zero-point energy (~7B modes per B-Skyrmion) partially cancels classical binding. Essential for realistic nuclear binding energies. B=4: 6.99 MeV (theory) vs 7.08 MeV (experiment).
- **Authors**: S.B. Gudnason, C. Halcrow
- **Journal**: Phys. Lett. B 850, 138526 (2024)
- **DOI**: 10.1016/j.physletb.2024.138526
- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.09272

### [13] Carbon-12 in the generalized Skyrme model
**Tags**: `BPS`, `composition`, `Skyrme`
- **Summary**: Sextic term + pion mass correctly reproduces alpha-clustering in B=12 Skyrmions. Energy orderings of triangular vs chain configurations preserved.
- **Authors**: (arXiv:2401.08778)
- **Journal**: arXiv:2401.08778 (2024)
- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.08778

### [14] Generalized skyrmion crystals with applications to neutron stars
**Tags**: `BPS`, `Skyrme`, `binding`
- **Summary**: Sextic term governs high-density EoS. Asymptotically stiff (v_sound = c). Neutron star models match NICER/LIGO data.

## I. Gravity, Emergent Metric, and Soliton Self-Gravity

### [34] Effective metric from field theory normal modes
**Tags**: `gravity`, `non-local`, `Skyrme`
- **Summary**: Linearizing ANY classical field theory around a soliton background generically produces an effective curved spacetime for perturbations. Key formula: g_eff^{μν} = -∂²L/∂(∂_μφ₀)∂(∂_νφ₀). Pure L₂ gives FLAT metric; L₄ and L₆ give nontrivial curvature.
- **Authors**: C. Barcelo, S. Liberati, M. Visser
- **Journal**: Class. Quantum Grav. 18, 3595 (2001)
- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/gr-qc/0104001

### [35] Emergent geometries and nonlinear dynamics in photon fluids
**Tags**: `gravity`, `experimental`
- **Summary**: EXPERIMENTAL demonstration of emergent curved spacetime from nonlinear density waves in photon fluid. Self-induced background flow creates acoustic metric. Shock = curvature singularity. True backreaction observed.
- **Authors**: F. Marino, D. Vocke et al.
- **Journal**: Nature Scientific Reports 6, 23282 (2016)
- **DOI**: 10.1038/srep23282
- **URL**: https://www.nature.com/articles/srep23282

### [36] Derrick's theorem in curved spacetime
**Tags**: `gravity`, `Skyrme`
- **Summary**: Gravity weakens but cannot eliminate Derrick instability. Skyrme term still essential even with gravity. Modified virial: I₃ = I₁ + 2I₂.
- **Authors**: S.B. Gudnason, M. Speight
- **Journal**: Phys. Rev. D 100, 025014 (2019)
- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/1906.00702

### [37] Universal self-gravitating Skyrmions
**Tags**: `gravity`, `Skyrme`
- **Summary**: Exact analytical solutions of Einstein SU(2)-Skyrme on S³×R. Two-branch structure (Skyrmion + Bartnik-McKinnon) merges at α²_max ≈ 0.0404. Beyond this, no regular soliton exists.
- **Authors**: R. Vera
- **Journal**: arXiv:2503.03872 (2025)
- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.03872

### [38] Skyrmion black hole hair / Aharonov-Bohm protection
**Tags**: `gravity`, `Skyrme`, `composition`
- **Summary**: B=1 Skyrmion around Schwarzschild BH = first counterexample to no-hair for global charges. Baryon number measurable via AB phase shift. Topology must resurface when BH evaporates.
- **Authors**: G. Dvali, A. Gussmann
- **Journal**: arXiv:1605.00543, arXiv:1611.09370 (2016)
- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/1605.00543

### [39] BPS Skyrme model as neutron stars
**Tags**: `gravity`, `BPS`, `Skyrme`
- **Summary**: BPS energy-momentum = perfect fluid. Maximum NS mass ~ few M_solar, R ~ 10 km. L₆ stiffens EoS at high density. Without L₆, too soft for observed 2 M_solar neutron stars.
- **Authors**: C. Adam, C. Naya, J. Sanchez-Guillen, A. Wereszczynski
- **Journal**: arXiv:1407.3799 (2014); Phys. Rev. D 2015
- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/1407.3799

### [40] Gravitating Skyrmions with fermions (negative mass)
**Tags**: `gravity`, `Skyrme`
- **Summary**: Self-gravitating Skyrmions + Dirac fermions with Yukawa coupling. NEGATIVE ADM mass solutions exist: regular, asymptotically flat, anti-gravitating. Spectral flow of fermion eigenvalues.

- **Journal**: arXiv:2407.17504 (2024)

### [41] Pion stars in gauged Einstein-Skyrme
