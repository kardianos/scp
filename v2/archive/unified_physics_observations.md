# Core Observations for Unified Physics Theory

## Immutable (Must Satisfy)
- Speed of light (c) constant/invariant.
- Conservation: energy, momentum, charge, angular momentum.
- Gravity: attractive, ~1/r², weakest force, universal.
- Particle spectra: masses, charges, spins (e.g., electron 0.511 MeV, proton 938 MeV).
- Nuclear stability (e.g., periodic table), fusion/fission.
- Electromagnetic phenomena: Coulomb, spectra, polarization.
- Relativity: E=mc² equivalence, time dilation (GPS/clocks).
- Cosmology: expansion (Hubble), CMB uniformity, element abundances (Big Bang).
- Galaxy rotation curves, lensing (DM effects).

## Modifiable (Theory-Linked)
- Quantum probabilities/uncertainty (deterministic alternatives?).
- Wave-particle duality (emergent?).
- Entanglement/non-locality (geometric?).
- SM symmetries (SU(3)×SU(2)×U(1); geometric unification?).
- Infinities/renormalization in QFT.
- Hierarchy problem (masses).
- Dark energy (5-68% universe).
- Black hole info paradox.
- Arrow of time/entropy increase (emergent?).
