### The Conceptual Bridge: From Cosmic Motion to Particle Physics

The core idea is that the "implicit handed bias" (`ξ` or the vector `Cµ`) is not a fundamental, God-given constant. Instead, it is an **emergent field generated by the large-scale, coherent motion of mass-energy**.

This is perfectly analogous to electromagnetism:
*   A static charge creates an electric field.
*   A **moving charge (a current)** creates a magnetic field.

In our Chiral Harmonic Particle Theory (CHPT):
*   A static mass creates a static density gradient (gravity).
*   A **rotating mass (a matter current)** should generate a dynamic, chiral field bias.

This means the vacuum is not empty; its properties are dynamically determined by the distribution and motion of the matter within it. This is very much in the spirit of Mach's Principle and General Relativity.

### How it Works: The "Chiral Frame-Dragging" Effect

The rotation of a massive object like a galaxy or star would "drag" the spacetime field along with it, imprinting a local rotational "texture" onto the vacuum. This texture is the chiral bias. A soliton (particle) existing within this textured vacuum would feel this bias.

This leads to a **hierarchical model of chiral bias**:

2.  **The Galactic Field (`C_galactic`):** A spinning galaxy is a colossal, coherent vortex of matter. This would generate a much stronger, local chiral bias field that permeates the entire galaxy.
