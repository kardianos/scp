## OPEN ISSUES (pinned, need future work)

### Pin 1: α=1.2 not 1.0 (gravity exponent)
See OPEN_ALPHA_1.2.md. Eight candidate fixes identified.
Most promising: self-sourcing depletion, asymmetric coupling.

### Pin 2: Mass emergence mechanism
The Lagrangian mass m=1.5 provides confinement but is a parameter.
The depletion integral ∫δρ could be the gravitational mass, but
the self-limiting mechanism needs work. Harmonic resonance hypothesis
was rejected. Need: a mechanism where mass = equilibrium consumption
rate, set by the braid's topology.

### Pin 3: EMF from complex fields
T10G showed complex braids carry U(1) charge. T10H showed global
charge doesn't produce force (need gauge field). Next step: promote
U(1) to gauged symmetry (add A_μ) and test if charged braids interact
via 1/r Coulomb. Risk: gauge coupling might kill the braid (V24-MA showed
this for oscillons).

### Pin 4: Two-braid attraction
T7 showed repulsion at D=30 (beyond Yukawa range). Need test at D=10-15
(inside range). Also: two-braid test WITH M7 depletion — does the
depletion gradient produce attraction even when direct force is repulsive?

### Pin 5: Topological protection
Winding oscillates dynamically (T4b). Structure is robust but winding
metric is noisy. Need: volume-integrated topological charge instead of
single-line phase measurement. Or: upgrade to complex/SU(2) fields for
true π₃ topology.

### Pin 6: Substrate (T9)
Untested. What micro-structure produces V(P) = V(det F)?
Need: lattice coarse-graining, expansion cosmology test.
Connected to: vacuum structure, background field properties.

### Pin 7: No spontaneous braid formation
T10D (Kibble-Zurek) showed braids don't form from cooling.
The μ<0 potential drives toward uniform high-energy state, not defects.
Need: different cooling protocol, or different potential, or accept
that braids are metastable (require specific creation mechanism like
collision/annihilation).
