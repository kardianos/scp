# HFKT v13: Algebraic Variations of Entanglement

The objective of V13 is to define how fractionally charged geometric patterns (quarks) can combine to form a stable integer-charge knot (Baryon). The failure of the explicit 3-knot vibration simulation raises a critical question: **How are these field regions mathematically entangled in the first place?**

There are three primary algebraic variations for entangling distinct cores into a unified $Cl^+(3,0,1)$ field:

## 1. The Product Ansatz (Weak Entanglement)
*The method utilized in the early V13 numerical simulators.*
Instead of adding vectors (which tears the $S^3$ manifold geometry), the topology of three distinct knots ($R_1, R_2, R_3$) is combined via point-wise geometric quaternion multiplication:
$R_{Composite}(\vec{x}) = R_1(\vec{x}) \otimes R_2(\vec{x}) \otimes R_3(\vec{x})$

*   **Mechanism:** The twists (phases) of the field multiply natively. Topological Baryon numbers are perfectly additive ($B_{total} = B_1 + B_2 + B_3$).
*   **Physical Implication:** The knots remain fundamentally independent algebraic structures. The energy is minimized when they are together, but if kinetic energy or asymmetric tension occurs, they can drift infinitely far apart without breaking their individual $S^3$ topologies. This perfectly predicts the **expulsion of the D-knot** observed in Avenue 4. *This ansatz fails to produce Quark Confinement.*

## 2. The Rational Map Polynomials (Strong Entanglement)
Rather than placing three separate knots in the same room, we define a *single unbroken algebraic structure* that inherently features 3 distinct "lobes" or roots in physical space.

By mapping $\mathbb{R}^3$ onto the complex plane $\mathbb{C}$ via stereographic projection, a multi-knot can be defined by a single rational polynomial:
$W(z) = \frac{z^3 - c}{z^3 + c}$

*   **Mechanism:** The polynomial $W(z)$ natively dictates 3 distinct geometric roots (representing the 3 quarks: UUU, UUD, etc., by altering the coefficients).
*   **Physical Implication:** The three cores are intrinsically bound by the algebraic rigidity of the polynomial. You cannot pull one root to spatial infinity without catastrophically warping the entire background field equation across all space (costing infinite energy). **This algebraic variation natively enforces Color Confinement.** The quarks are mathematically inseparable because they are features of a single monolithic formula, not three adjacent objects.

## 4. Retarded-Time Phase Resonance (Light-Cone Entanglement)
*A philosophical realignment to Pure Process Monism.*

The previous models (1-3) treat the knots as static spatial geometric objects. But in V13, **matter is not an object embedded in the field; it is a continuous *process* of the field.** The knot does not "move"; the knotting distortion *propagates* through the medium. The fundamental constraint is causality: $ds = c \cdot dt$.

*   **Mechanism:** Because the field only communicates at exactly $c$, any topological twist affects surrounding space with a strict, non-negotiable speed-of-light delay. 
*   **Physical Implication:** Entanglement is not a static geometry, but a **perfectly timed, self-sustaining echo chamber**. 
    *   For three propagating field twists to stably co-exist, the distortion wave propagating *from* core A must arrive at core B at the exact moment core B is undergoing the matching phase of its own cycle. 
    *   They are "bound" together because if they drift apart, the strict $c \cdot dt$ time-delay is broken, the phase-locked resonance collapses, and the destructive self-interference causes the local topological energy to violently dissipate. 
    *   The illusion of "non-local" binding is created by perfectly synchronized local arrival of delayed waves from the other cores.

## 5. Solitonic Flux-Tube Breathing (Dynamic String Tension)
As a topological twist propagates ($\nabla \times R \neq 0$), it forces the adjacent "vacuum" regions of the $S^3$ manifold to counter-twist to maintain the overall boundary conditions. 

*   **Mechanism:** When three fractional knots (say, U, U, D) propagate in parallel through the medium, the phase incompatibilities (Red, Green, Blue offsets) between them prevent the interstitial vacuum from returning to the $R=1$ quiescent flat state.
*   **Physical Implication:** The space *between* the propagating twists is forced into a persistent line of topological tension—a dynamic flux tube. This is not an arbitrary $L_4$ Lagrangian patch we bolt onto the math. It is a natural consequence of the continuous field trying to "heal" itself while three mutually incompatible wave-processes propagate through it simultaneously. The tension of this unhealed flux tube is what physically drags a "minority" knot back toward the majority pair.
