# V24: The Particle as Propagating Self-Reconstruction

## Thesis

The v21-v23 oscillons were studied as STATIONARY objects sitting in vacuum,
slowly leaking energy through harmonic radiation. Every gravity mechanism
failed because the oscillon is a leaky resonator with no confinement.

This framing is wrong. A particle is not a standing wave in a medium. It is
not a fluid disturbance. It is not a bowstring vibration.

A particle is a **self-reconstructing process** that exists BECAUSE it moves.
The three-field alignment is not a static configuration — it is a dynamical
process that must be continuously re-enacted through spatial propagation.
Losses at any instant are irrelevant because the pattern is fully remade at
each step ds through space, and ds > 0 always, just as v < c always.

The stationary oscillon (v=0) is an idealization that does not exist in nature,
just as T=0 does not exist. The real particle is always moving, always being
reconstructed, and its stability comes from the reconstruction process, not
from the static binding energy.

## The Core Ideas

### 1. Self-Alignment as Process, Not State

The three fields φ₁, φ₂, φ₃ in the oscillon naturally phase-lock through the
triple-product coupling P = φ₁φ₂φ₃. V23-A confirmed this: the antisymmetric
(misaligned) modes are HARDENED (more massive than vacuum), meaning the system
resists misalignment.

But in v21-v23, self-alignment was treated as a FACT of the initial condition.
The symmetric oscillon was initialized with φ₁ = φ₂ = φ₃ and the alignment
was taken for granted.

**What if alignment is a PROCESS that must be continuously maintained?** Like a
gyroscope that needs spin to stay upright, or a bicycle that needs motion to
stay balanced. At v=0, the alignment process stalls, and the oscillon slowly
loses coherence (the v21 energy leakage). At v>0, the alignment is
continuously refreshed by the propagation through the field medium.

The analogy: a spinning top falls over when it stops spinning. The "spin" of
the oscillon is its motion through space.

### 2. Motion as the Mechanism of Existence

A stationary oscillon is a standing wave made of two counter-propagating
traveling components. These components leak at every reflection (harmonic
radiation). The standing wave is inherently lossy.

A MOVING oscillon is a traveling wave packet that interacts with the vacuum
ahead of it and leaves a wake behind. At each spatial step ds:

1. The leading edge of the oscillon encounters fresh vacuum (φ = 0)
2. The triple-product coupling pulls the three fields into alignment
3. The aligned configuration is the oscillon — it has been RECONSTRUCTED
4. The trailing edge releases the vacuum back to φ = 0

The reconstruction happens at the speed of the massive field: v_max = c.
The oscillon can move at any v < c. At each step, the full pattern is
remade from the vacuum ahead.

**Losses are irrelevant** because the pattern is not "the same material" at
each instant — it is a process, like a flame propagating along a fuse, or
a nerve impulse along an axon. The fuse burns and is consumed; the flame
persists. The oscillon radiates and is consumed; the reconstruction persists.

### 3. The ds > 0 Requirement

Just as:
- A massive particle cannot reach v = c (Lorentz factor diverges)
- A massive particle cannot reach T = 0 (zero-point energy, third law)

A massive particle cannot reach ds = 0 (complete stasis). There is always
some spatial displacement per unit time, some minimum motion.

This minimum ds corresponds to:
- The de Broglie wavelength: λ = ℏ/p (the reconstruction length)
- The Compton wavelength: λ_C = ℏ/(mc) (the minimum reconstruction step)
- The Zitterbewegung: rapid oscillation at ω = 2mc²/ℏ even at "rest"

The Dirac equation predicts that a "stationary" electron actually trembles
(Zitterbewegung) at the Compton frequency. This trembling IS the minimum ds.
The particle is never truly at rest — it always has spatial motion, and this
motion maintains its existence.

For the oscillon: the breathing mode (ω_breath ~ 0.95m) IS the Zitterbewegung.
It is the internal ds that maintains the oscillon even when the center-of-mass
velocity is zero. The breathing is not a "vibration of a thing" — it is the
thing itself, the self-reconstruction process operating in the rest frame.

### 4. Why the Stationary Oscillon Leaks

In v21, the stationary oscillon loses energy at dE/dt ~ -10⁻⁵. This is
BECAUSE the stationary oscillon is trying to exist at the impossible limit
ds_COM = 0. The breathing provides internal ds (Zitterbewegung), but the
harmonics at 2ω, 3ω radiate because they exceed the mass gap.

In a moving frame (v > 0), the Doppler shift modifies the harmonic content:
- Forward direction: harmonics blue-shifted → higher above gap → radiate
  more efficiently in forward cone
- Backward direction: harmonics red-shifted → some drop BELOW gap →
  cannot radiate → energy retained

The net effect: at nonzero velocity, some harmonics are Doppler-shifted
below the mass gap and become trapped. The radiation loss DECREASES with
increasing velocity.

At some critical velocity v_c, ALL harmonics are Doppler-shifted below the
gap in the backward direction, and the oscillon becomes PERFECTLY STABLE.
This is the minimum ds for the self-reconstruction process.

### 5. Three-Field Precession

For a stationary oscillon: φ₁ = φ₂ = φ₃ = f(r)cos(ωt). All fields identical.

For a moving oscillon (velocity v along z): the Lorentz boost mixes the
temporal oscillation with spatial structure. Each field acquires a
DIFFERENT spatial phase:

    φ_a(x, t) = f(r') cos(ω't - k_a · x + δ_a)

where k_a depends on the field index a and the velocity. The three fields
are no longer identical — they PRECESS relative to each other.

This precession has an angular momentum: the SPIN of the particle. The
spin arises from the motion-induced phase difference between the three
aligned fields. At v = 0, no precession, no spin. At v > 0, the three
fields precess at a rate proportional to v, giving spin.

The triple product P = φ₁φ₂φ₃ for the precessing triad has a different
spatial structure than for the static triad — it may have a helical
character, winding around the direction of motion. This helicity is the
particle's spin projection along the momentum axis.

### 6. Confinement from Reconstruction

The v23 failure was: no confinement → energy leaks → gravity mechanisms fail.

If the particle is a self-reconstruction process, confinement has a different
meaning. Energy doesn't "leak" from a propagating flame — the flame IS the
continuous conversion of fuel to heat. Similarly, the oscillon doesn't "leak"
energy — it converts vacuum (φ=0 ahead) into oscillon (φ≠0 at the core) into
wake (φ≈0 behind).

The "strong force" in this picture is the alignment force: the triple-product
coupling that pulls three fields into synchrony. The strength of this force
determines how efficiently the vacuum is converted into oscillon at each step.

The "weak force" could be the FAILURE of alignment: when the three fields
don't perfectly synchronize (partial misalignment), some energy is lost in the
reconstruction step. This is analogous to beta decay — a partially-aligned
state is metastable and decays.

The "residual force" (gravity analog) would be the modification of the
vacuum by the passage of the oscillon — the wake it leaves behind, which
affects the reconstruction of subsequent oscillons passing through the
same region.

## Testable Predictions

### Test 1: Boosted Oscillon Stability

**Hypothesis**: A moving oscillon (v > 0) has a LOWER radiation rate than a
stationary one (v = 0).

**Method**: Take the equilibrated v21 oscillon profile. Lorentz-boost it to
velocities v = 0.1, 0.2, 0.3, 0.5, 0.7, 0.9. Evolve in 1D for t = 10000.
Measure dE/dt at each velocity.

**Expected**: dE/dt decreases with v. At some v_c, dE/dt → 0 (perfect
stability through motion).

**Null result**: dE/dt is independent of v (or increases). This would mean
motion doesn't help stability, and the self-reconstruction picture is wrong.

**Code base**: Modify v21/src/triad1d.c to initialize with a boosted profile.
The Lorentz boost of a scalar field: φ(x, t) = f(γ(x - vt)) cos(γ(ωt - vkx))
where γ = 1/√(1-v²).

### Test 2: Harmonic Doppler Shift

**Hypothesis**: The Doppler shift moves some harmonics below the mass gap.

**Method**: For the boosted oscillon at each v, compute the DFT power spectrum
in the BACKWARD direction (frequency as seen by a co-moving observer behind
the oscillon). Check if the 2ω harmonic drops below m.

**Expected**: At v > v_c where 2ω·√((1-v)/(1+v)) < m, the second harmonic
can't radiate backward. This gives v_c = (4ω² - m²)/(4ω² + m²).
With ω = 0.87, m = 1.0: v_c = (3.03 - 1)/(3.03 + 1) = 0.50.
Prediction: oscillons become more stable above v ≈ 0.5c.

### Test 3: Thermal Bath Stabilization

**Hypothesis**: An oscillon in a thermal bath (T > 0) is more stable than
in vacuum (T = 0), because the thermal fluctuations provide the minimum ds
needed for self-reconstruction.

**Method**: Add a stochastic force to the v21 evolution:
    ∂²φ_a/∂t² = ∇²φ_a - m²φ_a - ∂V/∂φ_a + η_a(x,t)
where η_a is Gaussian white noise with ⟨η_a(x,t)η_b(x',t')⟩ = 2T·δ_ab·δ(x-x')·δ(t-t').

Scan T from 0 to 0.1 (in units of m). Measure oscillon lifetime at each T.

**Expected**: Lifetime increases with T (up to some optimal T*), then
decreases (too much noise destroys the oscillon). The optimal T* is the
"Goldilocks temperature" for self-reconstruction.

**Null result**: Lifetime monotonically decreases with T (noise always hurts).
This would mean thermal fluctuations are purely destructive, not constructive.

### Test 4: Three-Field Precession Under Boost

**Hypothesis**: A boosted oscillon develops phase differences between the
three fields (precession), and this precession is connected to stability.

**Method**: Boost the oscillon. Track φ₁(0,t), φ₂(0,t), φ₃(0,t) at the
center of the moving frame. Compute the phase differences
    Δθ₁₂(t) = arg(φ₁) - arg(φ₂)
    Δθ₂₃(t) = arg(φ₂) - arg(φ₃)

**Expected**: At v = 0, all phases equal (no precession). At v > 0, phases
differ and rotate at rate ∝ v. The precession stabilizes the oscillon.

### Test 5: Wake Structure

**Hypothesis**: A moving oscillon leaves a "wake" (modified vacuum) behind it
that has a different field structure than the undisturbed vacuum.

**Method**: Boost an oscillon. After it passes through a region, measure the
residual field φ(x) in the wake region (behind the oscillon). Compare with
the undisturbed vacuum.

**Expected**: The wake has a small but nonzero field structure that decays
slowly (power-law, not exponential). This wake is the "gravitational" residual
— it modifies the trajectory of subsequent oscillons passing through the same
region.

## What Changes from V23

V23 studied STATIC oscillons and their PAIRWISE interactions. Everything
leaked, nothing was confined, and all gravity paths failed.

V24 studies MOVING oscillons and their PROCESS of self-reconstruction. The
key shift:

- V23: "The oscillon IS a configuration. Does it leak?"
- V24: "The oscillon IS a process. Does it self-sustain?"

The first test (boosted oscillon stability) is the simplest and most
decisive. If dE/dt decreases with v, the entire V24 framework is supported.
If not, we need to understand why motion doesn't help before proceeding.
